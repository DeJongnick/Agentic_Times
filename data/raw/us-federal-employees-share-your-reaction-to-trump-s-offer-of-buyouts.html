US federal employees: share your reaction to Trump’s offer of buyouts

Date : 2025-01-29T17:59:51Z   |   Auteur : Guardian community team   |   Journal : US news

Since Donald Trump took office, he has made efforts to radically reduce the civil service.

An email was sent to the entire federal workforce – about 3 million people – in January offering buyouts, and Elon Musk’s Department of Government Efficiency (Doge) has stripped out government contracts and taken aim an entire departments.

We want to hear from you. Are you a federal worker who has been laid off or impacted by the layoffs? Have you been rehired? How has your life and work been affected?
