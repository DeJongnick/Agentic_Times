The best Christmas travel gifts: 38 presents for explorers and armchair travellers

Date : 2024-12-09T07:00:15Z   |   Auteur : Rachel Dixon   |   Journal : Life and style

Not sure what to get friends and family for Christmas? Fear not, we have plenty of ideas for a last-minute present.

Our carefully chosen selection of travel-inspired gifts ranges from the practical to the indulgent, with great books for the armchair traveller and outdoor gear for the active.

Maybe a cool new suitcase for weekends away would suit, or a stylish eco wash kit designed for travel? For those who have it all, perhaps opt for an experience to remember. Vouchers for vineyard visits, hotel breaks or afternoon tea may hit the spot. Happy shopping … and happy holidays!
