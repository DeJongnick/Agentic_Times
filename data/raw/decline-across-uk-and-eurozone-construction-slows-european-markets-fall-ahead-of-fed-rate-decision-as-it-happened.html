The headline construction purchasing managers‚Äô index from S&P Global edged up to 46.6 in April from 46.4 in March, signalling the slowest decline in output in three months. Any reading below 50 points to contraction.

UK construction companies have endured a bumpy ride since the start of the year as domestic economic headwinds and hesitancy among clients led to a lack of new work to replace completed contracts.
