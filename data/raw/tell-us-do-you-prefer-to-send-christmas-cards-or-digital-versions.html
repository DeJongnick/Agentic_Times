Tell us: do you prefer to send Christmas cards or digital versions?

Date : 2024-12-11T15:22:07Z   |   Auteur : Guardian community team   |   Journal : Life and style

We’d like to find out if you’re sending Christmas cards this year.

There’s been a suggestion that gen Z who have grown up with all things digital still like to send physical Christmas cards. 59% of gen Z and 62% of millennials prefer physical cards over digital ones, according to a survey of 2000 adults by OnePoll and Shutterfly.

Now we’d like to hear from you. Do you like to send physical Christmas cards – or are you wary of the cost or environmental impact? Tell us about it below.
