Share your experience of renewing your vows

Date : 2025-08-19T09:23:54Z   |   Auteur : Guardian community team   |   Journal : Life and style

Couples eager to celebrate their love with their friends and family a second time around often choose to renew their wedding vows. We’re interested in finding out more about people’s experiences and why they decided to do it again.

How did you celebrate and how did it compare to your first wedding? Perhaps your original wedding plans were affected by the pandemic. How did the vow renewal affect your relationship afterwards?
