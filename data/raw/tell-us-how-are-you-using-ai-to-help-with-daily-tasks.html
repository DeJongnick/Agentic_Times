Tell us: how are you using AI to help with daily tasks?

Date : 2025-01-22T15:27:05Z   |   Auteur : Guardian community team   |   Journal : Technology

There’s so much that has already been said about artificial intelligence, its risks, and how it might completely reshape society.

But something that’s less discussed is the role AI tools are already playing in people’s daily lives – whether it’s to help them organize their time, or speed up otherwise arduous tasks at work.

As AI tools become more ubiquitous, we want to know how you might be using them, what you would be most interested in learning more about and any concerns you may have.
