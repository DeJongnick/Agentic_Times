Good landings with a woman in the cockpit

Date : 2025-05-02T17:14:38Z   |   Auteur : Auteur inconnu   |   Journal : Society

It’s disappointing to see that there is still such a gender gap for pilots (‘Oh, you’re a woman!’ Why are more than 90% of pilots still men – and can anything narrow the gender gap?, 1 May). In a wildly unscientific survey based on my holiday flights, my sweeping generalisation is that female pilots are preferable for their excellent landing skills, whereas men tend to just dump the plane on the runway with abandon. If you are a British Airways pilot flying to Italy this month, please ignore the above – I applaud your skills.
Anne Cowper
Bishopston, Swansea

• I too abandoned wearing a bra doing lockdown and very quickly realised how much more comfortable I felt (The one change that worked, 28 April).
