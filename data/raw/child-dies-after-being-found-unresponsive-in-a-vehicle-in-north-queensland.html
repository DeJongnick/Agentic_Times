Child dies after being found unresponsive in a vehicle in north Queensland

Date : 2024-11-25T07:21:50Z   |   Auteur : Mostafa Rachwani   |   Journal : Australia news

A child has died after being found unresponsive in a vehicle in north Queensland.

Police officers were called to a vehicle in Innisfail about 1pm on Monday after reports a child had been found unresponsive.

Emergency services provided medical assistance but the child was declared dead.

Investigations are continuing and a report will be prepared for the coroner.

The temperature at Innisfail Aero weather station reached a high of 30.4C on Monday, according to the Bureau of Meteorology.
