Emma Chisit? Check your Karen Vallance

Date : 2025-10-03T16:58:47Z   |   Auteur : Auteur inconnu   |   Journal : Books

And not only mondegreen subtitles (Letters, 2 October). In the spirit of Emma Chisit, I approached my newsagent with my Guardian vouchers to pay what I owed. In a broad Australian accent, he asked my address and looked up his ledger. “Karen Vallance?” he enquired. “Oh no, no, my name’s Mary!” “Yeah I know that! I’m askin’ do yer want ya current balance?”
Mary Weber
Eastbourne, East Sussex

• Unfortunately, all wills in the UK are not public documents post-probate (Letters, 26 September). That is only true of those in England and Wales. I can recommend the official gov.uk probate record download service, which makes English and Welsh wills available 14 days after probate has been granted for a very commendable £1.50 each.
Peter Johnson
Leicester
