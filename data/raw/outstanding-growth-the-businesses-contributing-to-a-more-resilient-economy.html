Outstanding Growth: the businesses contributing to a more resilient economy

Date : 2025-10-06T19:28:58Z   |   Auteur : Auteur inconnu   |   Journal : Telstra Best of Business Awards: 2025

These small and medium-sized businesses are doing meaningful work – from striving to solve homelessness, to aiming to improve our cognitive performance – and demand for their services has led to significant growth.

Get to know the State Winners in the Outstanding Growth category of the 2025 Telstra Best of Business Awards.

EMPACT GROUP – Australian Capital Territory winner

EMPACT GROUP offers a fresh take on a classic business consultancy. Its founder, Claire Thomas, believes in learning from those she’s helping, and co-designing sustainable solutions together with clients. The business strives to onboard the right people for each challenge, knowing that positively supporting the sectors the nation relies on most, including government, is a good thing for everyone.

SAVVY Beverages – New South Wales winner

Nutritionist Mark Curry wants to feed our way to optimal cognitive performance. After swapping coffee for his own blend health drinks, the former Sydney lawyer felt enhanced mental wellbeing and performance. He realised there was a gap in the market so he set up SAVVY Beverages, collaborating with other nutritionists to develop a diverse range of products to enrich minds and bodies with nutrients that help people do more and feel better.

Stedmans Construction and Engineering – Northern Territory winner

Established in 1989 by Michael Stedman, Stedmans Construction and Engineering is a remote-area construction company based in Maningrida, Arnhem Land. It boasts a fleet of trucks and machinery as well as a skilled team of plumbers, electricians, carpenters and labourers. The business upskills Territorians and delivers quality building works in environments where few others operate.

Real Time Monitoring Cloud – Queensland winner

Real Time Monitoring Cloud delivers fully integrated monitoring and response solutions and facility management. Smart, cloud-based monitoring tools flag or help fix challenges that disrupt the smooth running of business operations, from ineffective nurse-on-call systems to failing commercial fridges. The company is focused on improving safety, visibility and system performance for facilities in aged care, healthcare, retail and hospitality.

Claridge Construction – South Australian winner

Claridge Construction is a high-end custom construction company that collaborates with clients to create their dream homes, taking into consideration the specific challenges of the South Australian climate. From small beginnings, it has grown into a business with a large team of professionals, focusing on building excellence, high-quality outcomes and open relationships with clients.

Blackley Pipelines & Irrigation – Tasmanian winner

Blackley Pipelines & Irrigation creates sustainable water solutions for Tasmania’s future.
Its team of technicians uses cutting-edge materials and technology to provide pipeline installations, system maintenance, and tailored irrigation solutions designed to maximise efficiency within the agricultural, industrial and municipal sectors. The business serves farmers, councils and industries across the state.

We Are Mobilise – Victorian winner

We Are Mobilise wants to prove that homelessness in Australia can be solved. It is taking a direct approach to helping people doing it tough, by providing fast, flexible funding that goes to people in need to cover bills, rental costs and other living expenses, and provide safety and stability. The organisation is also developing employment pathways to support long-term change.

Revo Fitness – Western Australian winner

Revo Fitness is an independent gym with affordable memberships and no hidden fees, designed to give people the opportunity to boost their health. Self-guided workouts and welcoming environments make fitness simple and approachable. Revo started out with just one WA studio and now offers more than 60 locations Australia-wide, with more opening later this year.

Congratulations to all the State Winners achieving outstanding growth and contributing to Australia’s economy.
